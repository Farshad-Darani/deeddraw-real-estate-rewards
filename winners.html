How Winners Are Selected

Complete transparency in our drawing process ensures fairness for all participants

Fair & Transparent Process

Our drawing process is designed with complete transparency and fairness at its core. Every step is verified and documented to ensure integrity.

Entry Verification

Before the draw, all registrations are reviewed to ensure eligibility and fairness. Duplicate or invalid entries are removed, guaranteeing a fair competition.

  • Comprehensive eligibility verification
  • Duplicate entry detection and removal
  • Identity and payment confirmation
  • Final participant list validation

Automated Fair Selection

Our drawing utilizes advanced automated, unbiased selection software to ensure complete fairness and transparency for all participants.

  • Automated, unbiased selection software
  • Equal chance for all registered participants
  • No human intervention in selection process
  • Mathematically verified randomness

Live Drawing Event

The entire drawing is conducted live with complete transparency, covered by local media outlets and streamed across multiple platforms for maximum visibility.

  • Live streaming on multiple platforms
  • Local media coverage and attendance
  • Real-time winner selection
  • Complete process recording

Winner Verification & Announcement

Winners are thoroughly verified and then publicly announced through various media channels and platforms to ensure complete transparency.

  • Public announcement across all platforms
  • Media coverage and press releases
  • Social media and website publication
  • Community notification channels
