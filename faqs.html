Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our Real Estate Rewards Program

How are points calculated in the program?

Points are calculated based on your real estate transaction volume:

  • 1 point = CAD $500,000 in transaction value
  • Transactions must be completed and documented
  • Points are awarded after verification of transaction completion
  • Both residential and commercial transactions qualify

For example: A CAD $1,500,000 transaction would earn you 3 points.

What types of transactions qualify for points?

Qualifying transactions include:

  • Residential property sales (single-family homes, condos, townhouses)
  • Commercial property sales (office buildings, retail spaces, industrial properties)
  • Land sales and development transactions
  • Investment property transactions
  • Rental property acquisitions (for qualifying categories)

Note: Transactions must be legitimate, arms-length deals with proper documentation.

How long do I have to accumulate points?

Points accumulation is ongoing until the drawing is triggered:

  • Points accumulate from program launch until 400 total points are reached
  • No expiration date on earned points
  • Drawing occurs automatically when 400 points threshold is met
  • New program cycle begins after each drawing

How much does it cost to register points for the drawing?

Registration fees are structured as follows:

  • CAD $2,000 per point to enter the drawing
  • You can register as many or as few of your earned points as you choose
  • More registered points = higher chance of winning
  • Payment can be made via secure online processing

Example: If you have 5 points, you can register anywhere from 1-5 points for CAD $2,000-$10,000.

Can I register points in installments?

Yes, our flexible registration system allows:

  • Register points as you earn them or all at once
  • Multiple payment options available
  • Secure payment processing for all transactions
  • Immediate confirmation upon successful registration

You maintain full control over how many points to register and when.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept multiple secure payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Bank transfers (online banking)
  • Certified cheques
  • Wire transfers for large amounts

All payments are processed through secure, encrypted systems.

What are the prize amounts and how many winners are there?

The total prize pool is CAD $500,000 distributed as follows:

  • 1st Prize: CAD $300,000 (1 winner)
  • 2nd Prize: CAD $50,000 each (2 winners)
  • 3rd Prize: CAD $20,000 each (5 winners)

Total: 8 winners sharing CAD $500,000 in prizes.

How is the drawing conducted?

Our drawing process ensures complete fairness:

  • Conducted by certified independent auditors
  • Random selection using verified drawing systems
  • Live-streamed drawing for transparency
  • Official documentation of all results
  • Winners notified within 24 hours

When does the drawing take place?

The drawing is automatically triggered when:

  • 400 total points are registered across all participants
  • All registration fees have been collected
  • Verification processes are complete

Participants receive advance notice of the scheduled drawing date and time.

Who is eligible to participate in the program?

This program is restricted to licensed real estate professionals only:

  • Real Estate Agents & Brokers: Licensed professionals with verified credentials
  • Developers: Property development companies with business registration
  • Sales & Marketing Professionals: Real estate marketing and sales teams with employment verification
  • Mortgage Brokers: Licensed mortgage and financing professionals

All participants must provide professional license or employment verification during registration.

Do I need to be licensed to participate?

Professional licensing or verification is required for all participants:

  • Agents & Brokers: Must hold valid real estate license
  • Mortgage Brokers: Must hold valid mortgage broker license
  • Developers: Business registration and development permits required
  • Sales & Marketing: Employment verification with licensed real estate firm or brokerage

This is a B2B professional rewards program. All participants must demonstrate professional affiliation with the real estate industry.

Are there geographic restrictions?

Currently, the program is available for:

  • Canadian residents and businesses
  • Transactions within Canadian provinces
  • Licensed professionals operating in Canada

International expansion may be considered for future program cycles.

Are there any hidden fees or additional costs?

No hidden fees - our pricing is completely transparent:

  • Only cost is CAD $2,000 per point registration
  • No processing fees or administrative charges
  • No membership fees or annual costs
  • Winners receive full prize amounts (taxes may apply)

What happens if I win? How are prizes distributed?

Prize distribution process:

  • Winners notified within 24 hours via phone and email
  • Official verification and documentation process
  • Prize payment via certified cheque or wire transfer
  • Winners responsible for applicable taxes
  • Public announcement with winner's consent

How do I submit my transaction information for points?

Transaction submission is simple:

  • Log into your account dashboard
  • Click "Submit Transaction" and fill out the form
  • Upload required documentation (contracts, closing statements)
  • Our team verifies and awards points within 3-5 business days

Required documents vary by transaction type and participant category.

How do I track my points and registration status?

Easy tracking through your account:

  • Real-time points balance in your dashboard
  • Transaction history with verification status
  • Registration status for each point
  • Progress toward drawing threshold
  • Email notifications for important updates

What if I have technical issues with the website?

Our support team is here to help:

  • Email support: support@deedraw.com
  • Phone support during business hours
  • Live chat feature (when available)
  • Comprehensive help documentation
  • Video tutorials for common tasks
